This section covers fonts, heading tags, heading classes, body copy, and blockquotes.

To use these two font weights just extend their placeholders where you need them. All heading tags are %arial-bold-text while everything else is %arial-text.

Font Weights
  • %arial-text - Arial 400
  • %arial-bold-text - Arial Bold 600
  • %montserrat-text - Montserrat 400
