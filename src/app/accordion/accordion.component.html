accordion

Our base accordion has all the styles stripped out. It's just a container with an arrow that changes positioning for open and close. By default the accorions are closed but we have highlighted in the _accordion.scss file what you need to comment out and comment in to switch that around. There are also additional syles you can add on to make the accordion look like a mixture of panel accordions, examples below.

The reason for the additional list-unstyled on the ul class="accordion__wrapper" is to clear the padding and list style type of an unordered list. The .accordion__warpper turns the ul into a flex parent with a direction of column, and also sets a max width of 650px with 0 margins.

Paneled Accordion

To make your accordions look like panel accordions just add onto the accordion base styles. Add --paneled onto .accordion, .accordion__title, and .accordion__container.
