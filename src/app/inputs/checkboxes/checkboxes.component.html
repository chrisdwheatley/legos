Checkboxes Groups

In our forms checkboxes are stacked by default. Just add a the .checkbox class around a parent container for the inputs.

<div class="checkbox">

  <label>

    <input type="checkbox" value="" >Checkbox Option 2

  </label>

</div>
Inline Checkboxes

Similarly use .checkbox--inline class on a series of checkboxes that you want to appear alongside eachother.



<label class="checkbox--inline">

  <input type="checkbox" id="" value=""> 2

</label>

<label class="checkbox--inline">

  <input type="checkbox" id="" value=""> 3

</label>
