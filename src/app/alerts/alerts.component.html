alerts

Global Alerts

These are alerts that get fixed to the top of the screen and stay until they are dismissed with the close button alert__close. I would have an example here but it would just stay fixed to the top and that doesn't seem nessisary.

Markup:
<div class="alert alert--global alert--danger" role="alert">
...
</div>
Notifcations

These alerts that fade into an area of the viewport and a smaller text area, normally in the top corner and then fade out after a certain amount of time. For these notifications you can customise their placement sby adding your own position class to it. Recommended: top right

Markup:
<div class="alert alert-notification alert--danger" role="alert">
...
</div>
Oh snap! Class alert--success.

Colors
  • alert--danger
  • alert--success
  • alert--info
  • alert--warning

These colors control the alerts backgroud color, font color, and when alert--notification is used a border color.
